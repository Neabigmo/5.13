# Publication Strategy

## Candidate Venues

- Machine Learning.
- TMLR.
- JMLR.
- COLT or related theory venues if the theorem package becomes sufficiently strong.

## Contribution Requirements

The paper must clearly distinguish:

- general stability calculus;
- exact k-NN perturbation characterization;
- finite metric separation witnesses;
- computational evidence versus formal minimality proof;
- compatibility with classical consistency.

## Risks

- Prior work may already imply parts of the general calculus.
- Witness minimality may remain computational rather than formal.
- Tie-breaking assumptions may narrow perceived contribution.
- Consistency discussion must avoid overstating worst-case finite examples.

## Response Strategy

Maintain a literature table, novelty assessment, proof audit notes, and explicit scope statements.
