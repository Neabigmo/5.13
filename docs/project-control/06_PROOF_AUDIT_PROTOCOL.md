# Proof Audit Protocol

Codex must audit every proof and claim before it enters the paper as theorem-level material.

## Checklist

- Quantifiers are correct.
- Definitions match `02_DEFINITIONS_SPEC.md`.
- Pointwise, expected, and uniform claims are not confused.
- Worst-case and distributional claims are not confused.
- Computational evidence is not written as proof.
- Tie-breaking assumptions are explicit.
- Duplicate points and conflicting labels are handled or excluded.
- Fixed k and sequence k_n are not confused.
- Classification and regression are not mixed.
- Classical consistency results are not contradicted.

## Labels

Use `Theorem`, `Lemma`, `Proposition`, `Claim under review`, or `Conjecture` honestly. Uncertain results remain under review.
